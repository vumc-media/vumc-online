One church. More than one doorway.

VUMC Online is not separate from our church campus. It is another doorway into the same worship, the same mission, and the same community. This space is here to help people stay connected beyond Sunday morning, engage in ministry from anywhere, and participate in the life of Versailles United Methodist Church no matter where they are.
